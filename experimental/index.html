Sketchbook


A "sketchbook" of short animation experiments, based on my travels during a study-abroad program in Rome. My goal was to imitate the process of sketchbooking, but using animation mediums: to work quickly and loosely, take stylistic risks, and combine multiple materials.


Responsible for all aspects.
