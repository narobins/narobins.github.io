Tree

I wrote this Blender plugin to general procedural models of trees, and used the outputs in a series of illustrations that also included ink paintings, digital drawings, and graphics generated with programs I wrote in Processing. This was a self-directed project; my primary goal was to reconciliate two ways of working: procedurally and by hand.
